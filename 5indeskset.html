5" deskset

Product Line Renewal

Category Product design
My Role Product Designer
Year 2018



01

Overview

This was an in-house design project to renew the Snom deskset series for a new 5-inch model.


Challenge

Inheriting the signature brand language of Snom and adopting the latest design trend


Solution

Implementing the look and feel, keypad layout and icons of the old Snom models and applying new forms and colour combinations to the new design



02

Design Trend Research

After defining the signature design language of Snom, I started to look for the references in terms of modern workplace products, form transformation and material finishing, which might be the inspirations for ideation.
