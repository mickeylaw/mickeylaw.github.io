Dect Base

Product Line Renewal

Category Product design
My Role Product Designer
Year 2017



01

Overview

In this project, my job was to redesign the VSP dect base in order to make it align with the Snom design language, since the last generation was an outsourced design prior to VTech's acquisition.


Challenge

Based on the design brief that my product manager provided to me, there were three criteria to achieve:

1. A striking dect base design with Snom design language

2. The look and feel should be sleek and sophisticated

3. It can sit on the desk and be wall-mounted



02

Form Research

Since the design of form was crucial, I started to do form research, looking for sleek and sophisticated designs that could be the inspirations.
