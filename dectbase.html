Dect Base

Product Reskin Project

Category Product design
Brand Language
My Role Product Designer
Duration 2 weeks
January 2017



01

Overview

In this project, my job was to redesign the VSP dect base in order to make it align with the Snom design language, since the last generation was an outsourced design prior to VTech's acquisition.


Challenge

Based on the design brief that my product manager provided to me, there were three criteria to achieve:

1. A striking dect base design with Snom design language

2. The look and feel should be sleek and sophisticated

3. It can sit on the desk and be wall-mounted



02

Form Research

Since the design of form was crucial, I started to do some form research, looking for some sleek and sophisticated designs that could be the inspirations.



03

Ideation

I sketched as many ideas as I could using the images that I collected from the form research as reference.


Fixed stand


Flexible stand

The concept of rotating stand was chosen as user could change the angle of the dect base from standing to flat effortlessly. This feature also facilitated the wall mount function, which did not require an extra piece of wall mount bracket, minimising the manufacturing cost.



04

Development


Problem

After building the 3D model in CAD, this idea did not work out as expected due to the obvious unwanted gap at the hinge. The integrating part of the body and stand should be more seamless and smooth. I then went through the whole ideation process from scratch in order to solve this problem.


Solution

Within a day, I did more idea sketches and came up with a new idea using a detachable magnetic stand to resolve the gap problem.



05

Final Design


Transformable Stand

The stand can be attached to the body with either side to facilitate different purposes.


Look and Feel

The design is simple and clean, using the white LED button as the accent.
