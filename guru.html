Guru

VR Buddhist Training Companion

Category Conceptual Design
Designer Mickey Law
Year 2018

Guru is a VR Buddhist training companion for buddhists, lay devotees in particular, to have regular daily practice at home, hence promoting Dana(generosity) , Viriya(vigour) and Dhyana(meditation). As a buddhist, one should always bear in mind the Three Jewels: the Buddha, the Dharma which is the teachings of Buddha, and the Sangha, which is the monastic community. Guru improves home training experience of lay devotees by providing a VR personal ascetic training hall, online sutra lectures and connections with the monastic community.



01

Brainstorming

I used Buddhism as the main topic to brainstorm initially. This process helped me extend and develop my thinking in an organised manner. Through the brainstorming session, I uncovered the difficulties and needs of lay devotee to practice Buddhism in daily life.



02

Concept


VR Buddha Hall

In the VR Buddha hall, user can pay respect to the Buddha and meditate(Dhyana) in front of him. Meanwhile, user can interact with the singing bowl in VR and create sounds which the brain waves can synchronise with to generate a healing effect and clarity of mind.

Sutra Lib

Guru provides sutras for user to chant and allows them to transfer the merit for their families and friends by connecting to the social media, as a mean of Dana.

Dharma Hall

User can attend online sutra expounding sessions which the words from Buddha are taught and explained by the Buddhist priests. After the lecture, discussion session is provided to helps lay devotees consilidate their learning and achieve Viriya in Buddhist practice.
