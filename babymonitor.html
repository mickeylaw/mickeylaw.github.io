Baby Monitor

Product Reskin Project

Category Product design
Brand Language
My Role Product Designer
Duration 2 weeks
April 2019



01

Overview

In this project, our team was asked to renew the design of the VTech baby monitor. I was responsible for the fixed camera monitor design.


Challenge

1. Adapting the latest design trend

2. Optimal range of rotation angle to accommodate both sitting and wall mount purposes.



02

Design Trend Research

Initially, our team worked together to define the latest design trend. Each of us was responsible for one category and we gathered all the moodboards to review if the images were relevant to the others. Below are the key trends that I chose for ideation.



03

Ideation

I sketched my ideas based on super strong geometry, soft extrude, cut contrast and seamless.



04

Final Design


Super Soft Geometry

The body, neck and base are smoothly blended together to demonstrate a super soft look and feel.


Seamless

The front cabinet and back cabinet are assembled with minimal width of gap, providing a seamless and clean outlook.
